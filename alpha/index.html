Triggers

Rules

  • When the cursor enters the boundaries of the box, the image switches from grayscale to full color.

  • The image also zooms in slightly, giving a feeling of focus.

Feedback

  • This is a visual microinteraction so the user see the changes instantly, the image shifts from muted grayscale to vibrant color.

  • The image enlarges slightly, creating a sense of motion and highlighting the hovered box.

  • Users may feel a sense of interactivity since the hover makes the element feel alive and engaging.
