Rules

Degree card Hover On

  • The image switches from grayscale to full color.

  • The image also zooms in slightly

  • The card background color changes to gradient

  • cursor changes to pointer

Degree card Hover Off

  • The image switches from full color to grayscale.

  • The image also zooms out slightly to default.

  • The card background color changes from the gradient to default.

  • cursor changes back to arrow

+ button Click to open

  • "+" icon rotates 135 degree to make an "x" icon

  • menu bar slides open to the right

x button Click to close

  • "x" icon rotates back to make a "+" icon

  • menu bar slides to close

"favorite" button Click

  • button gets filled in black

  • undo the black fill in

Share button Click

  • Send-like motion occur and the button appears to be flying out

Flip button Hover On

  • button gets filled in yellow from left to right

Flip button Hover Off

  • button gets unfilled from right to left

Flip button Click

  • Flips the degree card horizontally to show other side

  • Flips the degree card horizontally to default
